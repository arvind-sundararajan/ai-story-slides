Company research agent at work

The company-research-agent is a multi-agent tool that generates comprehensive company research reports by leveraging a pipeline of AI agents to gather, curate, and synthesize information about any company. The platform features multi-source research, gathering data from various sources including company websites, news articles, financial reports, and industry analyses. It utilizes AI-powered content filtering, using Tavily's relevance scoring for content curation, and asynchronous processing, with an efficient polling-based architecture for tracking research progress.
