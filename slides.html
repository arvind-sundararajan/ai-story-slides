Introduction

Bisheng was created for enterprise AI scenarios.

Bisheng is an open LLM application devops platform, focusing on enterprise scenarios, and has been used by a large number of industry leading organizations and Fortune 500 companies. The platform features Lingsight, a general-purpose agent with expert-level taste, which is achieved through the Agent Guidance Language framework, embedding domain experts' preferences, experience, and business logic into the AI. This framework allows for the creation of complex enterprise application scenarios, providing a high level of customization and flexibility. Bisheng's unique workflow provides an independent and comprehensive application orchestration framework, enabling the execution of various tasks within a single framework.

Bisheng's workflow is designed to handle complex scenarios such as multi-type input/output, report generation, content review, and more. The workflow allows for human intervention and feedback during execution, supports loops, parallelism, batch processing, conditional logic, and free combination of all logic components. This level of complexity is made manageable through a user-friendly and intuitive interface, with operations like loops and parallelism easily visualized as a flowchart. The platform's ability to handle complex scenarios and provide deep optimization suggests a high level of performance, making it suitable for a wide range of enterprise applications.

The platform is designed for enterprise applications, including document review, fixed-layout report generation, multi-agent collaboration, policy update comparison, support ticket assistance, customer service assistance, meeting minutes generation, resume screening, call record analysis, unstructured data governance, knowledge mining, data analysis, and more. Bisheng supports the construction of highly complex enterprise application scenarios and offers deep optimization with hundreds of components and thousands of parameters. The platform provides enterprise-grade features, which are the fundamental guarantee for application implementation. This level of customization and flexibility makes Bisheng a valuable tool for organizations looking to implement AI solutions in their operations.

The target audience for Bisheng is likely enterprise organizations looking to implement AI solutions in their operations. The platform's focus on enterprise scenarios and its ability to handle complex applications make it a good fit for organizations with complex operational needs. The platform's user-friendly interface and ability to provide deep optimization also make it a good fit for organizations looking to implement AI solutions without requiring a high level of technical expertise. Overall, Bisheng is a powerful tool for organizations looking to implement AI solutions in their operations, and its flexibility and customization options make it a valuable asset for a wide range of industries.
